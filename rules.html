How To Play The Game

1) Name of a person in the form of dashes will be displayed on the screen.

2) You have to input an alphabet one by one.

3) If that alphabet would be present in that name, it will be displayed on the same position in dashes.

4) If the alphabet would not be present in that name, a character of title would be turned into red.

You have to try to complete the name untill all characters of title turns red.
